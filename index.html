Current Work Experience

Computer Vision Engineer

Jan 2020 - Current Robotic Lab- SRTTU

Projects: Work on vision of Waste Segregate Robot; Developing Android Application to control a new costumized Treadmill.

Activities: I work as a computer vision engineer in Robotic Lab under supervision Dr. Rahmani in Mechanical Engineering Department. I work on automatic waste resegregation. The goal of project, in my section, is about designing vision of robot to detect and track objects automatically such as PET (polyethylene terephthalate). Recycled polyethylene terephthalate is known as RPET, and it is the most widely recycled plastic in the world. Generally, We aim to design and develop Robot which segregate these kind of plastic as human done before.

Our Computer Vision team, including me,have costumised, one of the most popular state-of-the-art Object Detection by transfer learning and, using Object Tracking like,Deep Sort, method to find PETs among wastes in first phase. Furthermore, We work on Camera Calibration, finding, and mapping the object's center point in Live recording. We use various industrial cameras in order to do the project. It is worth noting that I work with two control/ electrical engineers to build our control system due to communication with robot's movement engines. In order to do this, data is transferred by serial ports.
